- POS billing and sales management
- Product, barcode, and inventory management
- Purchase and supplier management
- Supplier return and sales return handling
- Customer management
- Expense tracking
- Low stock alerts
- Profit, sales, and business reports
- AI reorder assistant and stock decision support
- License Generator for paid software activation
